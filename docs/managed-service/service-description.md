## Managed Assessment Service

**Find out what's actually exploitable before attackers do.**

### What We Do

OdinForge runs a fully automated adversarial assessment against your external-facing infrastructure. We execute the same attack chains that real threat actors use — application exploitation, credential harvesting, cloud privilege escalation, container breakout, and lateral movement — and deliver a sealed proof package of every confirmed vulnerability.

No agents to install. No platform to manage. We attack, you get the report.

---

### How It Works

1. **Scope** — You tell us what to hit (domains, IPs, cloud environments). We send a brief scoping questionnaire.
2. **Attack** — Our AEV engine executes a 6-phase breach chain against your targets, from initial application compromise through full lateral movement.
3. **Deliver** — You receive a sealed Engagement Package with five components:

| Component | Audience | What's Inside |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| **CISO Report** | Executive / Board | Risk Grade A-F, business impact, compliance implications |
| **Engineer Report** | Security Engineering | Full chain trace with HTTP evidence, remediation diffs per finding |
| **Evidence JSON** | SIEM / Automation | Machine-readable findings for ticket creation and tracking |
| **Defender's Mirror** | SOC / Detection | Sigma, YARA, and Splunk SPL rules for every attack we executed |
| **Breach Chain Replay** | Anyone | Self-contained HTML — step-by-step visualization of the entire attack path |

---

### What Makes This Different

**Every finding has real evidence.** No inferred vulnerabilities. No theoretical risk. If we report it, we exploited it and captured the HTTP request, response, and status code. Findings without proof are suppressed from your report automatically.

**Six-phase breach chain, not a scan.** We don't just find CVEs — we chain them. Application compromise leads to credential extraction, which leads to cloud IAM escalation, which leads to container breakout. You see the full blast radius.

**Detection rules included.** Every attack we execute generates corresponding Sigma/YARA/Splunk detection rules. You leave the engagement with both a list of what's broken and the rules to catch it happening again.

**One engagement, one price.** No subscription lock-in. No per-seat licensing. Engage when you need validation, pay for what you use.
